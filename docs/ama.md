# Ask Me Anything (AMA)

Have questions about the training content, prompt engineering, or AI use at AmaDema? Submit them here!

!!! info "How This Works"
    - **Anonymous:** No name or email required
    - **Private:** Only the instructor sees your questions
    - **Public answers:** Selected questions will be answered on the [FAQ page](faq.md)
    - **Always available:** Submit questions anytime during or after the workshop

## Response Time

- **During workshop:** Instructor may address questions in real-time
- **After workshop:** Questions answered within 2-3 business days
- **Complex questions:** May take longer, will appear on FAQ when ready

---



## Privacy Note

Your questions are:
-  Completely anonymous
-  Only visible to instructor
-  Never shared with identifying information
-  Used to improve training content

When answered publicly on FAQ page, questions are:
- Rephrased to maintain anonymity
- Combined with similar questions
- Sanitised to remove company-specific details if needed
